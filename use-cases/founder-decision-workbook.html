Use Case

Decision Workbook for Solo Founders

Founder decisions often mix product, time, reputation, and runway pressure. The workbook turns that mix into a written choice you can act on and review.

Solo founders make many decisions without a board, manager, or team lead to force clarity. That freedom is useful, but it also makes it easy to keep reopening the same choice instead of testing one direction.

Use it before a commitment

  • Choosing which feature or offer to build next.
  • Deciding whether to keep, pause, or kill a project.
  • Picking a pricing, positioning, or launch channel test.
  • Committing a fixed block of time to one growth bet.
The useful output is not certainty. It is a documented bet, the risk you are accepting, and the next evidence you will collect.

What the kit adds

Decision Clarity Kit gives you the decision intake page, option scorecard, premortem, action bridge, review pages, Markdown templates, and examples in one reusable pack. It is intentionally small so you can use it before momentum disappears.

Founder review loop

After seven or thirty days, review the original decision record. Separate what was known at the time from what only became obvious after the result. That review improves the next decision instead of turning the current one into another vague lesson.
