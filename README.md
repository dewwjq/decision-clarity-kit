## What The Full Kit Includes

The full kit is in prelaunch. It is prepared as a digital download with:

- 20-page printable English workbook
- 20-page printable Chinese workbook
- Markdown templates for Obsidian, Notion import, or any notes app
- filled English and Chinese example decision records
- option scorecard
- premortem worksheet
- action bridge
- 7-day and 30-day review pages
- local browser decision tracker with CSV/JSON export

## Use It When

- a decision keeps looping in your head
- you need to compare options without pretending everything is certain
- you want to record why you chose something before hindsight rewrites the story
- you want a simple review loop after the outcome becomes visible
