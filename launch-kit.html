Long post

Most hard decisions do not fail because you lacked opinions. They fail because you never wrote down what you believed before the outcome, what risk you accepted, and what would make you reverse course.

Decision Clarity Kit is a small workbook for that moment. It includes a decision intake worksheet, option scorecard, premortem, action bridge, review pages, Markdown templates, filled examples, and a local browser tracker.
